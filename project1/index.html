SETTING:
Nikke takes place in a post-apocalyptic future where the Earth was suddenly attacked and overrun by extraterrestrial mechanical creatures called Raptures that destroyed everything on the surface, forcing the remaining humans to take refuge underground and establish the Ark, a metropolis ruled by the Central Government. The story is played from the perspective of the Commander, who graduated from the Military Academy one day prior to the events of the game, and his squad of Nikkes as they endeavor to reclaim the surface. Despite their lack of experience, they perform extraordinarily in the operations and survive various encounters with rare Rapture species throughout the journey. They lead a squad called Counters, which consists of the Nikkes Anis, Rapi and later down the story, Neon. The Commander views Nikkes with sympathy and privately advocates for Nikke rights, which is illegal in accordance with the laws of the Ark. Outlaws may be sent to the Rehabilitation Center or the Outer Rim, an area fully isolated from the rest of the Ark, and they have formed terrorist organizations claiming to fight for human rights for themselves.

GODDESS OF VICTORY: NIKKE is a South Korean, immersive, third person, sci-fi, RPG shooter game. You the player command various maidens to form a beautiful but deadly squad that specializes in wielding guns and other unique sci-fi weapons.
