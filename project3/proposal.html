Project 3: Star Catcher

1. High Concept

Catch the stars as they fall from the sky and gain points. Dropping stars or catching the wrong star will cause you to lose lives until game over.


2. Genre

This game is a catcher game.


3. Platform

This game is for desktop web.


4. Aesthetics

The style will be pixel art


5. Gameplay

Stars will fall from the top of the screen. The player will control a character who will run along the bottom of the screen. (possibly have the ability to jump to higher platforms?) When the player collides with a star, they will "catch" it.
